Improvisation and joy are the cornerstones of Tony's style.

Tony studied Classical Cuban piano at the Ignacio Cervantes Conservatory in Habana, Cuba. Afterward, Tony turned to study the rhythms of jazz.

For over a decade, Tony has developed a jazz performance rich with influence of traditional Cuban rhythms.

Tony has taken his performances around the world in countries such as: Albania, Germany, Argentina, Belgium, Chile, Denmark, Spain, France, Holland, England, Norway, Portugal, Sweden, and Russia.
