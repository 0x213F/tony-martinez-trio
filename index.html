About

Personel

Tony's piano performances are rich with Cuban influence. Improvisation and joy are at the core of Tony's style. In moments of relaxation, you can catch Tony setting the vibe with a Bolero or Bossa Nova.

Tony has been playing piano professionally for over 25 years. Aside from his trio work, Tony plays in any setting. He has an extensive history playing in orchestral, small group, and solo settings.

The rhythm section is composed of two percussionists: Hanson Lee and Josh Schultheiss. They fuze their knowledge of jazz and contemporary music to keep the groove fresh and clean.
