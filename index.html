Improvisation and joy are the cornerstones of Tony's style. Tony's piano performances are rich with Cuban influence.

Tony is an international touring artist who has been performing professionally for over 25 years. Aside from his trio work, Tony plays in any setting. He has an extensive history playing in orchestral, small group, and solo settings.

Tony studied piano at the Ignacio Cervantes Conservatory in Habana, Cuba.
